# BIME 591 -- Biomedical Visualization Hello class! Welcome to BIME 591 -- Biomedical Visualization. This class is co-instructed by Graham, Uba, and myself, so please feel free to chat with any and all of us! In this course, we will cover some basic visualization theory/practices, go through biomedical visualization examples, interact with guest speakers, and design your own biomedical visualization. Here below is the current curriculum for the course. Although there are some papers, exercises, or lectures we think are "musts", we are also very open to adjusting the course content based on your feedback: 1.
